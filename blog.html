ORO Coin

Where Digital Gold Meets Stability

As a business designer, I embarked on an innovative project called ORO Coin. The goal? To create a stablecoin that seamlessly aligns with the ever-fluctuating gold price in the Iranian market. Imagine a digital currency backed by tangible gold, offering stability and liquidity. ORO Coin simplifies gold trading, empowering individuals to buy, sell, and hold gold effortlessly. Whether you’re a seasoned investor or a first-time trader, ORO Coin bridges the gap between traditional precious metals and the dynamic world of cryptocurrencies. Welcome to a new era of secure, accessible, and transparent gold trading.

Damino

Cultivating Care, Nurturing Growth

At Damino, we sow the seeds of innovation in the fertile soil of agriculture and animal welfare. Our mission? To empower farmers, pet owners, and veterinarians alike. Whether it’s streamlining farm operations, providing virtual vet consultations, or ensuring ethical practices, Damino is the bridge between tradition and technology. Welcome to a world where every harvest yields progress, and every pawprint leaves a legacy.
